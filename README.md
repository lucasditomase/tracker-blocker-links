# Tracker Blocker

Stop invisible scripts and cookies from following you across the web. Tracker Blocker enhances your privacy by blocking known tracking domains at the DNS level before they can even load.

### Key Features
• **Privacy Dashboard**: See detailed stats of blocked tracking attempts.
• **Incognito Experience**: Complements your browser's private mode system-wide.
• **Custom Filtering**: Add your own rules to block specific tracking scripts.
• **Native & Fast**: No third-party code; purely Swift-based protection.
